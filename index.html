I made this thinking on how there were card games where there were around 5-8 people and one person was a killer. This wiki explains that http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mafia_(party_game) I made this as a fun guess who did game which randomly chooses a killer and a set of clues and you are a detective with a certain amount of guesses.
